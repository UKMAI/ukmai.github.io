# WHO IS UKMAI?
United Kingdom for Moderation of Artificial Intelligence is a community of people concerned about the direction that modern AI is going and wish to inform the community.

## YOUR GOAL?
UKMAI wants to make people more aware of the consequences of unregulated AI and mindless investment into the technology.

The information on this website is designed to be shared to show people the error of their ways. To let people understand that this, and show they are not a minority.

The AI bubble makes it seem like the entire world is racing for AI, but it's not like that. The vast majority of people [^1] believe that AI companies are prioritising their business over the interests of their users.

To inform people, please see all the pages at the [index of UKMAI.GITHUB.IO](https://ukmai.github.io/).

Share these where you see fit, AI slop on Facebook, AI pranks on Instagram, mindless hype on LinkedIn. The consumer has the power, every dollar spent is a vote cast, and we don't have to vote for this. Make sure people know that.
